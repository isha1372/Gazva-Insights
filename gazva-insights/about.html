About Gazva Insights

What we publish, who writes for us, and the standard we hold ourselves to.

Gazva Insights is a publication covering Quran study, Fiqh, Islamic history, and reflections on everyday life, alongside a creative corner for stories and poems. We write in both English and Malayalam, for readers who want depth without unnecessary complexity.

What we publish

Our Quran section focuses on structure, language, and meaning. Fiqh covers rulings and the reasoning behind them, written so the "why" is never left out. Thareekh brings Islamic history to life through people and turning points rather than dates alone. General is where we publish reflections on character, family, and daily life. Kurippathukal is our creative space for short stories and poetry.

Our editorial standard

Every article is reviewed before publishing, with sources noted where relevant. When scholars differ on a question, we try to represent that difference honestly rather than flattening it into a single answer.
